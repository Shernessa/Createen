About Us



Shernessa Joseph, who is of Saint Lucian nationality, is a diligent 14-year-old student of the Vieux Fort Comprehensive Secondary School; one of the most prestigious secondary schools on island. She express herself through creative means such as writing, painting and photography among other things.

"As a teen photorapher in Saint Lucia, I felt that older folk, especially, overlooked my talent. I was rarely taken seriously when I approached other photographers asking to collaborate. I came to realize that I was not th eonly one suffering from this issue. I have witnessed a lot of talent being wasted and going unwasted. I decided to make a creativespace for teenagers which allows us to showcase our work, give each other feedback, help each other improve and ultimately, establish our place in our various industries."



We are located in Vieux Fort, Saint Lucia.
