Problem Statement:

There is a lack of appreciation for the arts in Saint Lucia especially when produced by teenagers. It is often overlooked and deemd as merely a "hobby" or "past-time".

Older folk fail to realize that teenagers can forge real careers from their talents.


Mission Statement:

To unite teenagers in the community, allow them to network and improve their skills so that they become comfortable with showcasing it.


Who Are We?

We are an organization which allows Saint Lucian teenagers to showcase their work, improve their skills and network with each other as well as bigger names in the local art industry.

We are inclusive of visual artists (photographers, videographers, graphic designers etc.), people involved in the performing arts (singers/songwriters, dancers, musicians etc.), content creators etc.
